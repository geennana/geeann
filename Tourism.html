White Beach, Boracay Island

White Beach on Boracay Island is a tropical haven boasting powdery white sands and crystal-clear waters. Its kilometers-long stretch offers a myriad of activities, from exhilarating water sports to leisurely beachfront dining. Renowned globally for its beauty, it captivates visitors with mesmerizing sunset views and a plethora of amenities, making it the epitome of a tropical paradise.

Ariel's Point

Ariel's Point, near Boracay Island, is an adventure hub with towering cliffs and clear waters. Here, thrill-seekers dive from various heights while snorkelers explore vibrant marine life. It's a breathtaking escape for adrenaline junkies and nature lovers alike.

Mount Luho Viewpoint

Mount Luho, Boracay's highest peak, provides stunning panoramic views of the island's beauty. Its trails offer vistas of pristine beaches, lush landscapes, and azure waters. Whether capturing photos, birdwatching, or simply enjoying the serene atmosphere, Mount Luho promises a tranquil retreat in nature's grandeur.

Puka Shell Beach

Puka Shell Beach, also known as Yapak Beach, is a serene escape from Boracay's crowds. With coarse sand, abundant puka shells, and crystal-clear waters, it's perfect for relaxation and sunset views. An untouched beauty, ideal for solitude and natural splendor.

Crystal Cove Island

Crystal Cove Island, near Boracay, entices adventurers with rugged landscapes, hidden coves, and vibrant marine life. Guided tours lead explorers through enchanting caves and snorkeling spots, unveiling the island's secrets. With breathtaking vistas and diverse attractions, Crystal Cove Island guarantees an unforgettable adventure for nature lovers and thrill-seekers.

Balinghai Beach

Balinghai Beach, on Boracay's northern coast, offers a secluded retreat for romance and relaxation. With golden sands, tranquil waters, and intimate ambiance, it's perfect for couples and discerning travelers seeking serenity.
