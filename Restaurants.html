Sunny Side Café

Located in Station 3, Boracay Island, Sunny Side Café offers a variety of delicious breakfast and brunch options, including Filipino favorites and international dishes.

Tilapia 'N Chips

Located in D'Mall, Boracay Island, Tilapia 'N Chips specializes in serving fresh tilapia cooked in various styles, alongside classic fish and chips.

Mama’s Fish House

Situated in Station 2, Boracay Island, Mama's Fish House serves fresh seafood dishes in a cozy and relaxed atmosphere, perfect for enjoying the island vibes.

Nami Resort Restaurant

Situated in Diniwid Beach, Boracay Island, Nami Resort Restaurant offers stunning ocean views and a menu featuring Asian fusion cuisine and fresh seafood dishes.

Los Indios Bravos

A popular restaurant and bar located in Station X, Boracay Island, Los Indios Bravos offers a diverse menu featuring comfort food, craft beers, and cocktails.

Salsa Fusion Restaurant

Salsa Fusion Restaurant in Malay serves up a tantalizing blend of Latin American and Filipino cuisine, creating a culinary experience that delights the senses. With its vibrant flavors, welcoming ambiance, and attentive service, it's the perfect spot for both locals and tourists looking to explore new culinary horizons.
